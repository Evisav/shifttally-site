# ShiftTally Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

## Introduction

ShiftTally is a mobile application developed and operated by **Event Vision AV, LLC** ("Company", "we", "our", or "us").

Event Vision AV, LLC is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how ShiftTally collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the ShiftTally mobile application.

ShiftTally is designed as a **local-first pay tracking application**, meaning most user data is stored directly on your device.

By using ShiftTally, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

---

## Information You Provide

ShiftTally may store information that you manually enter, including:

* Employer / Payee names
* Pay rates
* Shift and work history
* Overtime rules
* Deduction settings
* Tax configuration settings
* Earnings calculations
* Report data

This information is stored **locally on your device** and is not transmitted to Event Vision AV, LLC servers.

---

## Analytics Information

ShiftTally uses **Firebase Analytics** to collect limited, anonymous usage information, including:

* App usage patterns
* Feature usage
* Device type
* App version
* Session duration

This information is used to:

* Improve app functionality
* Identify usability issues
* Improve performance
* Guide future feature development

Firebase Analytics does **not** collect personal financial data entered into ShiftTally.

---

## Crash Reporting

ShiftTally uses **Firebase Crashlytics** to collect crash reports.

Crash reports may include:

* Device type
* OS version
* App version
* Crash stack trace
* Performance diagnostics

Crash reports do **not** include your shift or earnings data.

---

## Local Data Storage

ShiftTally stores your data **locally on your device**.

This means:

* Your data is not stored on Event Vision AV, LLC servers
* Your data is not shared with third parties
* Your data remains under your control

Deleting the app may result in loss of locally stored data.

---

## Cloud Sync

ShiftTally **does not currently support cloud synchronization**.

All data is stored locally on your device.

Future versions of ShiftTally may introduce optional cloud backup or sync features. This Privacy Policy will be updated if that occurs.

---

## Subscription & Billing

ShiftTally may offer subscription-based features.

All purchases are processed through:

* Apple App Store
* Google Play Store

Event Vision AV, LLC does not collect or store:

* Credit card information
* Billing addresses
* Payment details

Billing is handled by Apple and Google.

---

## Data Sharing

Event Vision AV, LLC does **not sell or share** personal data.

Limited anonymous data may be processed by:

* Firebase Analytics
* Firebase Crashlytics

---

## Children's Privacy

ShiftTally is not directed toward children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Because data is stored locally, device security is also important.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
